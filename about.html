About me

I'm a dynamic individual recently graduated from the Clark College Web Development program, where I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL. Beyond the realm of coding, I possess a keen eye for photography, capturing moments that tell stories through a lens. This artistic pursuit has not only refined my attention to detail but also nurtured my creativity, influencing my approach to design in the digital world.

I am very skilled in UX and UI design, where I combine empathy with aesthetics to create user-centric interfaces. My understanding of user behavior, coupled with a knack for aesthetics, allows me to bridge the gap between functionality and visual appeal effectively.

My journey also includes a significant chapter in the world of restaurant management. Through this experience, I developed impeccable organizational skills, learned the art of managing diverse teams, and mastered the delicate balance of customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. This background has instilled in me a strong work ethic, adaptability, and the ability to thrive under pressure with qualities that I carry into every endeavor.

I'm excited to merge my technical prowess from web development, artistic sensibilities from photography, and human-centered approach from UX/UI design, all while infusing the discipline and lessons learned from managing restaurants. I believe that this unique blend of skills allows me to approach challenges with a holistic perspective, find innovative solutions, and contribute meaningfully to every project I'm a part of.
